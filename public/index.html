Imani operator dashboard

Each row is a call. Fill in phone (with country code) and optionally a name and a scheduled time, then click Call. Leave the schedule blank to dial immediately. Scheduled times are interpreted in the participant's local timezone (inferred from the phone country code).

Names are used verbatim by Imani at call start — prefer the title + first + last form (e.g., Mr. Emeka Obi). Draft rows (light grey background) live only in this browser until you click Call. Submitted rows persist across refreshes.

Screening calls collect short answers to 12 questions and store them for an analyst to review. Click a row to send the participant to the Interview operator tab with phone + name pre-filled.
